We're here to help!

🚌

Find Your Bus

Check the bus schedule on PassioGo or Line Forty Nine.

🕒

Wait at the Bus Stop

Arrive a few minutes early and wait at the marked bus stop. You can always check how far the closest bus is.

🚶

Board the Bus

No need to pay a fare or anything, just walk on in.

🪑

Find a Seat

Find an open seat or hold onto a pole if the bus is crowded.

🚏

Request a Stop

Pull on the yellow line near the sides of the bus when near your destination to alert the driver.

📱

Exit the Bus

Gather your belongings and exit through the door nearest to you.
